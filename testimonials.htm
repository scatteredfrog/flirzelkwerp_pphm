Handyman Tips:

Did you know ...
if you're trying to hang something on the wall (a picture, or mirror), & are having a hard time measuring it exactly, this helpful hint may work. Put a small dab of toothpaste on the back of the picture in the center and press it up to the wall where you want to be. Take the picture away, & the toothpaste has marked the spot for the hook, & will wipe off easily.p.s. OOKs brand of hooks (from Home Depot) work very well for heavier objects.
