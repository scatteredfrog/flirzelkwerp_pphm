Handyman Tips:

Did you know ...
the most time consuming part of a caulking job is removing the old caulk. A can opener ( remember them?? They were what you used to open a big can of fruit juice for you non-dinosaurs ) will do a fine job of pulling out the old caulk. If you're recaulking a bathtub/shower, make sure and kill all the green microscopicytes (mold) with a bleach solution and allow everything to dry out (hair dryer) before recaulking. If there's a breeze near the doors or windows, heating bills are going the same way that gas prices have, and caulk may not stick to frozen bricks/siding.
